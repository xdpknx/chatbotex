Who is eligible? Citizen farmers How do I get this service? Visit the closest District Animal Production Offices with the animal production officer to register for the ostrich training with the supporting documents. The animal production officer will inform the applicant about the start of training and other details. What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of valid National Identity Card (Omang). How long will this service take? Immediate. How much does it cost? No cost.
