Who is eligible? Citizens and Non-Citizens. How do I get this service? The applicant must submit the following documents: Approved plans Application letter What supporting documents are required? Drawing of plans, layout and specifications of magazine approved by the Chief Inspector of Explosives as per the following requirements; Conditions regarding lightning conductor Requirements for magazine plans Magazine fence specification Application letter How long will this service take? 5 working days from the date of inspection. How much does it cost? There is an annual licence fee charged at BWP 2.00. Where can I obtain this service? Applications can be submitted at: Department of Mines P/Bag 0049 Gaborone Botswana Government Enclave, Block 6, Khama Crescent (Ministry of Basic Education building, floor 4) Tel. (+267) 365 7000 Fax. (+267) 395 2141 Email: mines [at] gov.bw Opening hours: 7:30 to 12:45 and 13:45 to 16:30, Monday to Friday, except public holidays.
