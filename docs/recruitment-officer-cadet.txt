Botswana citizens Aged between 18 and 24 years. On exceptional cases, age may be waived to recruit someone who is already holding a profession e.g. Medical Doctor Qualifications Minimum qualification for Officer Cadets is a Degree Requirements Candidate should possess a valid National Identity Card (Omang) or show valid proof of registration Candidates should be physically and mentally fit in accordance with the BDF Recruitment Medical Standards Minimum height of 165 cm for Males and 160 cm for Females Minimum body weight of 55 Kg for Males and 45 Kg for Females All candidates undergo the selection process, which include both physical fitness assessment and comprehensive medical examination Female candidates must not be pregnant. How do I get this service? Vacancies are advertised or published in local newspapers What supporting documents are required? Certified proof of qualification Valid National Identity Card (Omang) How long will this service take? Selection process takes 5 to 6 weeks How much does it cost? Free Where can I obtain this service?
