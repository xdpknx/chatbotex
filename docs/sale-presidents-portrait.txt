Who is eligible? Members of the Public, Private companies, Government departments. How do I get this service? Visit Information Services offices (selected offices). What supporting documents are required? No supporting documents are required. How long will this service take? Immediate. How much does it cost? BWP 2.00 per portrait. Where can I obtain this service? Selected offices of Department of Informataion Services across the country.
