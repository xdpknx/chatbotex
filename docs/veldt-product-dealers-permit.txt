The permit is valid for 30 days from date of issuance. Who is eligible? Citizens who are eighteen (18) years and above Residents with valid resident permits and passports, 18 years and above Non-residents with valid passports, 18 years and above How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit Veldt Products Dealers Permit Form to the nearest Department of Forestry and Range Resources office. What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of national identity card (Omang) Valid residential permit and passport for residents Valid passports for non - residents How long will this service take? Three (3) working days. How much does it cost? Citizens: BWP 50.00 Resident: BWP 100.00 Non-resident: BWP 300.00 Where can I obtain this service?
