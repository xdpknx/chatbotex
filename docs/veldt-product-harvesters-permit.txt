Veldt product means non-domesticated, vegetative biological resources that may be used for construction, medical, food, cultural activities and firewood. The permit is valid for 30 days from date of issuance. Who is eligible? Citizens who are 18 years and above. How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit Veldt Product Harvesters Permit Form. What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of valid National identity card (Omang). How long will this service take? Three (3) working days. How much does it cost?
