It also allows existing dealers to renew their licence. The licence is valid for three years. Who is eligible? Any person of or above the age of 21 Applicant should have no criminal record How do I get this service? Complete Form 13 (Application forms available online and at all police stations). What supporting documents are required? Valid National Identification documents Inspection report from Licensing Officer Trading licence Certificate of Incorporation (first-time applicants) Police clearance certificate (first-time applicants) How long will this service take? One (1) month How much does it cost? BWP 300.00 Where can I obtain this service? Any police station. Where can I get more information on this Service Any police station.
