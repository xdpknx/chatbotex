Examples of such adverts are legal notices, tender notices, change of name and change of land use. There are two types of gazette: Weekly Government Gazette which is published on Fridays Extra Ordinary Gazette is published any day of the week as and when required. The extra ordinary gazette is used to communicate urgent matters on the day to day running of the Government. Who is eligible? Government Ministries, Departments and Parastatals General Public Legal Practitioners / Law Firms How do I get this service? Submit a soft copy and hard copy of the notice to any of our bookshops. What supporting documents are required? Soft copy of the notice Hard copy of the notice How long will this service take? 9 days. How much does it cost? BWP 40 .00 per appearance.
