Who is eligible? Citizens and Non-Citizens eighteen (18) years and above How do I get this service? Any nearest Wildlife & National Park offices What supporting documents are required? Citizens- A certified copy of a valid National Identification Card (Omang) Non-Citizens- Certified Copy of Passport Certificate of the rifle Registration documents from the camps/lodges How long will this service take? Five (5) working days How much does it cost? Cost Duration Citizens BWP25.00 Bird hunting season in a year Non-Citizens BWP150.00 1 Week BWP300.00 1 month BWP750.00 1 Year Where can I get more information on this Service Department of Wildlife and National Parks Plot 50380 Moedi House, Fairgrounds Gaborone Botswana Tel.
