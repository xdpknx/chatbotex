Who is eligible? Citizens and non-citizens Recruitment establishments Recruitment associations How do I get this service? Download and complete the Recruiter’s Licence application form and submit with supporting documents to the nearest Labour Office Collection of Recruiters License after notification or applicant can check after service period What supporting documents are required? Company Registration Documents Tax Clearance Lease Agreement Returns of number of employees recruited in any district per quarter submitted within four weeks after each quarter with the Commissioner (only applicable for renewal application) Three (3) months Bank Statement (Minimum P10 000.00 balance) Identity Cards/ Work permits for Directors Copy of Workers Compensation Insurance Certificate List of potential Recruiter’s permit holders CVs of potential Recruiter’s permit holders How long will this service take? One (1) week How much does it cost? BWP20.00 fee for Recruiter’s licence Type A BWP100.00 for Recruiter’s licence Type B Where can I get more information on this Service Districts' Contact Numbers Department of Labour and Social Security Head office, Gaborone Tel.
