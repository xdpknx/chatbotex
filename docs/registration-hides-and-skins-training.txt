Who is eligible? Citizens 18 years and above. How do I get this service? Visit the nearest District Animal Production Office animal production officer to register for the hides and skins training with the supporting documents. The Animal Production Officer will inform the applicant about the start of training and other details. What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of valid National Identity Card (Omang). How long will this service take? Takes a few minutes. How much does it cost? No cost.
