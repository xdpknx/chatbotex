The request should be made three months prior to the event. Who is eligible? Institutions and the general public. How do I get this service? Submit a written request to the Commander Special Support Group (SSG) Indicate on the request letter the date, time and location of the event What supporting documents are required? Application letter Programme of events How long will this service take? Ten (10) working days How much does it cost? Charges are as follows; Event Type of service Charge Duration Extra Cost (per hour) Dinner dance Band P 4000.00 5 hours P 500.00 Wedding reception Band P 3 500 5 hours P 400.00 Any event Police traditional dance troop P 2000.00 5 hours P 400.00 Any event Military band P 2000.00 3 hours P 400.00 Any event Band hall hire P 5 000.00 1 day Where can I obtain this service?
