The donated blood is used to save lives. Who is eligible? Healthy individuals aged between 16 and 65 years. Weighing more than 50 kilograms (kg). A healthy person means any individual without an ailment that could be passed on to another person through blood transfusion. How do I get this service? Visit one of the donation centres and go through the screening process (hemoglobin, blood pressure & weight) and counselling. Participate during the National Blood Donation campaigns. What supporting documents are required? National identity card (Omang). Passport for non-citizens. How long will this service take? Immediate How much does it cost?
