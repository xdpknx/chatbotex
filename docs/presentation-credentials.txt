Arrangements will also be made for the Ambassador / High Commissioner to meet the Chief of Protocol and the Minister of International Affairs and Cooperation prior to the credential ceremony. The Department will issue a Note Verbale stating the procedure and requirements for the presentation of credential ceremony.
