This licence applies to those engaged in commercial fishing including those who own Fish Farms. Open fishing season is between March 1st and December 31st, annually. Who is eligible? Citizens eighteen (18) years of age and above How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit the Commercial Fishing License Form with supporting documentation What supporting documents are required? A certified copy of a valid National Identity card (Omang) Certified copy of boat registration How long will this service take? Five (5) working days How much does it cost? BWP 200.00 for 3 consecutive seasons Where can I obtain this service? Department of Wildlife and National Parks Plot 50380 Moedi House, Fairgrounds Gaborone Botswana Tel. (+267) 3971405 Fax.
