This license is valid for one year and therefore requires renewal annually. Who is eligible? Citizen and Non-Citizen. How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit Waste Management Facility License application form to the Department of Waste Management & Pollution Control Offices. What supporting documents are required? Location map and site plan. Environmental Assessment Report (Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) or Environmental monitoring procedure (Emp) or Environmental Management Plan (EMP), (Client to provide one of these documents with recommendation from DEA)). Operation plan. Certificate of Incorporation Lease agreement or title deed with site map Land use authorisation from local authorities (Zoning Permit) Environmental Health report Record keeping procedures Facility closure or rehabilitation plan How long will this service take? Ten (10) working days. How much does it cost? BWP 1000.00 licensing fee (charged per vehicle).
