This service caters for groups looking to spend more than a day at one of these National Parks, Game Reserves and Campsites. Who is eligible? Groups of Citizens and Non-Citizens Travel Agencies Mobile Tour Operators How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit a reservation request form through fax, surface mail and or email showing details of your reservation or visit the nearest office A notice will be sent to acknowledge your booking by mail or email, with an invoice attached asking for a full amount to confirm the reservation Your tour booking will be confirmed by surface mail or email In order to manage confirmation of the reservation, a payment request will be sent to obtain an upfront pay to secure the booking. Payment can be made by Cash, Credit Card, and Bank Cheques or in person at the office What supporting documents are required? Citizens: Certified copy National Identity Card (Omang) Non-Citizens: Certified copy Passport and Resident Permit Tour Operator Permit How long will this service take? Three (3) working days How much does it cost? Entrance fees 30 persons or less BWP 75.00 per day 30 persons to maximum of 50 BWP 100.00 per day Picnic fees 30 persons or less BWP 50.00 per day 30 persons to maximum of 50 BWP 75.00 per day Where can I get more information on this Service Department of Wildlife and National Parks Plot 50380 Moedi House, Fairgrounds Gaborone Botswana Tel.
