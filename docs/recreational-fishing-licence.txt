In Botswana, open fishing season is between March 1st and December 31st annually. Fishing is allowed in all water bodies. Who is eligible? Citizens and Non-Citizens who are 18 years of age and above. How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit Recreational Fishing License form with supporting documentation to any nearest Wildlife & National Park offices Those who wish to fish at the camps/lodges should bring the letter from the camp and for the dams they need the entrance permit from Water Utilities Corporation What supporting documents are required? Citizens: Certified copy of valid National Identity Card (Omang) Non-Citizens: Certified copy of Passport Letter from the Lodge/camp Permit from Water Utilities Corporation How long will this service take? Two (2) hours. How much does it cost? Monthly Annually Citizens BWP30.00 BWP360.00 Non-Citizens BWP300.00 BWP600.00 Where can I get more information on this Service Department of Wildlife and National Parks Plot 50380 Moedi House, Fairgrounds Gaborone Botswana Tel.
