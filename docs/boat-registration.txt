The service entails the following: Issuing registration certificates to all boats in Botswana. Issuing import permits to boats entering Botswana. Issuing inter-zonal permits to boats moving between zones. Zones are demarcated surface water bodies or rivers. Botswana is divided into six zones: Chobe, Okavango, Marico, Limpopo, Kwando Linyanti and Nata. Performing boat inspections and spraying. Who is eligible? All citizens, non-citizens and businesses who hold a Boat Ownership Certificate and own a boat can apply for this service. How do I get this service? Complete, and submit APPLICATION FOR BOAT REGISTRATION FORM Complete, and submit APPLICATION FOR BOAT REGISTRATION AND IMPORT PERMIT FORM The AVC unit will determine which form is needed based on the information provided by the customer The AVC unit will process the application and register the boat or spray the boat, based on the service requested What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of Omang card for citizens Certified copy of passports for non-citizens Boat Ownership Certificate How long will this service take? This process takes one day to complete. How much does it cost? Boat Import Permit: BWP44.00 Boat registration: BWP44.00 Inter-zonal Movement Permit: BWP44.00 Boat Inspection and Spraying: BWP44.00 Issue of duplicate certificate of registration: BWP2.00 Where can I obtain this service?
