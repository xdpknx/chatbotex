These are audit reports that members of the public are entitled to access. Who is eligible? Any member of the public can access these audit reports. How do I get this service? Interested persons need only to visit the Botswana Government Printers Bookshop, located at the below address and purchase a hard copy. Alternatively, the electronic copy may be downloaded on the forms attached. Botswana Government Printers Bookshop Finance House Government Enclave Gaborone Botswana Tel: (+267) 395-3202 / 390 4147 Fax: 395 9392 Opening hours: 7:30 to 16:30, Monday to Friday, except public holidays What supporting documents are required? Not applicable How long will this service take? This is a same day service, and the wait time at the office depends on the queue. How much does it cost?
