Who is eligible? Citizens and non-citizens Business. How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit Transboundary permit form to the Department of Waste Management and Pollution control. What supporting documents are required? Service agreement with disposer from importing country (Written agreement between the waste generator and waste recipient) Liability insurance from a local insurance company Motivational letter by the applicant stating reasons for export Certificate /licenses of the receiving disposal facility Material Safety Data Sheet How long will this service take? Fifteen (15) working days. How much does it cost? BWP 1000.00 licensing fee (charged per vehicle).
