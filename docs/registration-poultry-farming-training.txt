Who is eligible? Citizens. How do I get this service? Visit the nearest District Animal Production Office to register for the poultry farming training with the supporting documents. Animal Production Officer will inform the applicant about the start of training and other details. What supporting documents are required? Certified copy of valid National Identity Card (Omang). How long will this service take? Immediate. How much does it cost? No cost. Where can I obtain this service?
