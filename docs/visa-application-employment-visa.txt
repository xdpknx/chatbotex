This visa is issued with a validity period of six months and is extendable with justification. Who is eligible? All foreigners who require visas to enter Botswana are eligible for this service. How do I get this service? Go to any immigration office (contact information listed below), Botswana embassy or consulate to fill and submit Form 1 (Application for Visa in person. Submit the required documents (listed below) with the application form. Check the outcome of your application from the office where the application was submitted after seven (7) working days. An approval/rejection letter is sent to the office where the application was made. If the Visa application is approved, it will be printed at that office where the application was made or if not printed the applicant can travels with the approval letter as proof of a Visa to Botswana and the Visa will printed at the Airport/Immigration Ports of Entry. What supporting documents are required? The following documents are required to be submitted with the application form, where applicable: Certified copy of page 1 and 2 of passport showing validity period not less than six (6) months. Two (2) recent coloured passport size photos. Covering letter indicating reasons for visit. Flight itinerary and accommodation bookings or residential address in Botswana. A re-entry permit to the country where the applicant is ordinarily resident if such a permit is required by that country. Waiver from Department of Labour. Employment contract or confirmation letter of employment if applicable. Offer letter of volunteer position if applicable. Proof of financial means of support for volunteers if applicable. For extension of a valid visa - a letter of justification and a copy of previously issued visa. How long will this service take? It takes 7-14 working days. How much does it cost? The cost associated with this service is as follows: Single entry- for a period up to 3 months BWP500.00(US $50) Multiple entry- for a period up to 3 months BWP 1, 000.00(US$100) Multiple entry – for a period of up to six months BWP 1,500.00(US$150) Where can I obtain this service?
