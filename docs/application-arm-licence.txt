The licence is valid for five (5) years. Who is eligible? Any registered firearm owner How do I get this service? Complete Form 17 (Application form available online and at all police stations) Present the registered firearm before the Licensing Officer at the police station What supporting documents are required? Firearm Registration Certificate Arm licence Valid identification documents How long will this service take? One (1) working day How much does it cost? BWP 10.00 Where can I obtain this service?
