Examples of situations where individuals may pay by order of the courts include; Paying or receiving child support or spousal support. Paying fines issued by the court. Paying court fees. Providing deposits as required by the court. Who is eligible? Any individual involved in a case/s. How do I get this service? In order to pay or receive funds, individuals will need to provide: The case number of their case A valid National Identification card (Omang) for citizens. A valid passport for non-citizens. Payment can be made using cash only at any Magistrates Court. How long will this service take? It depends on the prescription of the order of court. How much does it cost? No cost Where can I obtain this service?
