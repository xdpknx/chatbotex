Who is eligible? Citizens and non-citizens. How do I get this service? Download, complete and submit a Licensing and registration of private operators of meteorological service application form to the Department of Meteorological Services. What supporting documents are required? Citizen- certified copies of national identity card (Omang) Non-citizen- certified copies of valid passport Submit general company documents (valid trade license, valid tax clearance certificate, certificate of incorporation, VAT Registration letter and declaration form) Completed site survey form How long will this service take? Thirty (30) working days. How much does it cost? No cost. Where can I obtain this service?
