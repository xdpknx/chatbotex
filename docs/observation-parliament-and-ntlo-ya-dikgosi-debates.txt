The Chambers have public galleries to sit members of the public. Who is eligible? Citizen and non-citizens How do I get this service? The service is obtained when: Parliament is sitting as follows:Official Opening Meeting (State of the Nation Address presented by His excellency the President) - November to December Sitting hours: 1400hrs to 1800hrs on Monday to Thursday 0900hrs to 1230hrs on Fridays Budget Meeting (Presentation of the Budget Speech by the Minister of Finance and Economic Development) - February to April Sitting hours: 1400hrs to 1800hrs on Monday to Thursday 0900hrs to 1230hrs on Fridays Winter/General Meeting (Discuss general issues) - July to August Sitting hours: 1400hrs to 1800hrs on Monday to Thursday 0900hrs to 1230hrs on Fridays Ntlo ya Dikgosi is sitting as follows: General meetings - January, June and October Sitting Hours: 0900hrs to 1630hrs on Monday to Fridays 1400hrs to 1630hrs on Wednesdays Where can I get more information on this Service Parliament of Botswana Public Relations Office Government Enclave P o Box 240 Gaborone Tel.
